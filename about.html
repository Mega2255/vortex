Our Mission

Empowering the world to access financial markets

Founded over 50 years ago, Riftvol has grown from a small derivatives desk into one of the world's leading online trading providers. Our focus has always been the same: give every trader — from complete beginners to institutional professionals — the tools, data and support they need to succeed.

We believe financial markets should be transparent, fair and accessible. That's why we've invested relentlessly in technology, education and customer service, while maintaining the highest standards of regulatory compliance and client protection.

Today, Riftvol serves over 400,000 active clients across more than 100 countries, offering access to 17,000+ instruments across forex, indices, shares, commodities, cryptocurrencies and ETFs.

Our History

Five decades of innovation

From a single dealing room to a global trading platform — the Riftvol story.

1974
Founded in London
Riftvol opens its first dealing room in the City of London, offering spread betting on gold and currency indices to private clients.
1995
Online trading pioneer
Riftvol launches one of the world's first online trading platforms, allowing clients to deal directly from their desktop computers.
2003
International expansion
Opening of offices in Australia, Germany and Singapore marks the beginning of our global footprint across 20+ countries.
2014
Mobile-first transformation
Award-winning iOS and Android apps launch, bringing full trading capability to smartphones for the first time at this scale.
2020
400,000 clients milestone
Riftvol reaches 400,000 active clients worldwide and is voted Best Finance App for the third consecutive year.
2026
Next generation platform
Launch of our rebuilt trading infrastructure with sub-millisecond execution, AI-powered signals and integrated TradingView charts.
